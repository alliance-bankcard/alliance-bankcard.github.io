About Us

Alliance Bankcard, LLC was formed in Utah, with a desire to change the way credit card merchant services are offered; eliminating the hard sell but emphasizing the exemplary service merchants should expect.

Alliance Bankcard began working in the credit card processing industry in 1999 and developed a substantial portfolio in Utah. In 2004 the company expanded to Michigan, where we continued to build our merchant base with an ultimate goal of expansion throughout the United States. Understanding the dynamics of a diverse, national, marketplace has placed Alliance Bankcard on the processing map.

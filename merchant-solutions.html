Merchant Solutions

Alliance Bankcard is a full service payment processing company. We offer convenient and reliable point-of-sale cash management solutions; including processing for all major credit cards, debit cards, competitive rates, internet processing, and electronic check services.

To compliment payment processing services we offer an array of products; equipment sales, leasing programs, toll-free 24 hour help desk, supplies, and more.

Why Choose Us

  • No hidden fees - We give you the clearest, most detailed statement in the industry
  • Next day funding - Get your money the next business day with a late cut-off time
  • American Express OptBlue® Program - Get one customer service contact, one statement, and one deposit for all card brands
  • Virtual terminal – Accept payments in the office or on the go quickly, easily, and securely
  • Free PCI – Absolutely no fees for merchants who complete PCI questionnaires
  • Competitive rates – Our rates are among the industry’s most competitive
  • Daily updates – Easy, stress-free reconciling with online reporting that’s updated daily
